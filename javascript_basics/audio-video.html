JavaScript Audio and Video Events

Video Events

        canplaythrough - Video can play through without buffering.
        playing - Video is playing. 
        timeupdate - displays the duration of the video being played.
        ended - Video playback ended.
        loadstart - Video load started.

Audio Events

         loadstart - Audio load started.
         playing - Audio is playing.
         canplaythrough - Audio can play through without buffering.
         timeupdate - Current audio time. 
         ended - Audio playback ended.
